A little about me:

I had my first brushes with the tech World while working as a FMCG operations manager. Working with PowerBI reports got me onto Excel, which led to Python, and then on to SQL.

Before long I found myself as the go-to in the team for anything tech related, and I decided it was time to plan a career change - a process which took me a little over a year as I finalized my MBA and outstanding work projects.

During that time, I kept developing my tech skills by taking online courses through Udacity, and I am now working through an entry- level software engineer program with _nology!

Tech aside, I mostly keep myself sane through sports, whether it be snowboarding, target-shooting or American Football, or travelling abroad.
